template<typename job_type>
struct std::formatter< kcenon::thread::typed_thread_pool_t< job_type >, wchar_t >

Specialization of std::formatter for wide-character typed_thread_pool_t<job_type>.

This enables formatting of typed_thread_pool_t<job_type> objects as wide strings using C++20 std::format.
